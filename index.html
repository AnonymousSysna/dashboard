Quick answers

Is this actually free? No hidden costs?

Yes, completely free. No credit card, no trial period, no upsell. I'm sharing my existing hosting setup at no cost to you.

What's the catch?

No catch. I'm covering the hosting cost myself. The only limits are the ones listed (content policy, inactivity, no heavy traffic). This is a personal project, not a business.

How long does setup take?

Usually within 24–48 hours after you message me, depending on my schedule.

Can I use a custom domain?

Not at this time. You get a subdomain on studentsite.dev or studentsite.online. DNS is managed on my end.

What happens if my site gets too much traffic?

I'll reach out first. If it's consistently overloading the shared server, we may need to take it down. This isn't designed for viral or high-traffic use cases.

Can I transfer my site to my own hosting later?

Yes. Static and PHP sites can be downloaded via FTP anytime. WordPress has export tools.
